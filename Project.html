Project-6: AstroPaper

AstroPaper is a beautifully crafted and highly customizable blog theme built on top of the Astro framework. With its type-safe markdown, super-fast performance, and keyboard and screen-reader accessibility, AstroPaper is the perfect choice for bloggers looking for a seamless and enjoyable writing experience.

This theme comes with light and dark modes, as well as customizable color schemes, ensuring that your readers will have a comfortable and personalized reading experience. AstroPaper also includes fuzzy search, draft posts, pagination, and a sitemap and rss feed, providing you with all the tools you need to create a professional and engaging blog.
